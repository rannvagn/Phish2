Distinguishing between scam emails and real ones is super important to stay safe online! Here are some easy tips to spot the fakes:

Check the sender: Make sure you recognize the sender's email address. If it looks weird or you don't know them, be cautious.

Watch Out for sketchy links: Don't click on any links unless you're sure they're legit. If you hover over a link, you can see where it leads. Look for anything fishy.

Don't panic: Scammers often try to scare you into acting fast. If an email seems too urgent or threatening, take a step back and think before doing anything.

Read carefully: Typos and weird formatting can be big red flags. Real companies usually spell things right and look professional.

Keep your info safe: Be careful about sharing personal or financial info online. Legit companies won't ask for this stuff over email out of the blue.

Be wary of attachments: Don't open attachments from emails you weren't expecting. They might contain viruses or other nasty stuff.

Double-check requests for money: If an email asks you to send money or make a payment, double-check with the company using their official contact info. Scammers love to trick people into giving away cash.

By following these tips, you can steer clear of scams and keep your online world safe and sound!
